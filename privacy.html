Privacy Policy
Last updated: January 2025  |  Version 1.0 Beta

Welcome

ShowUp ("we," "us," or "our") is a social TV tracking app that helps you track shows you're watching, save shows you want to watch, and share your viewing life with friends. We take your privacy seriously and want to be clear about what we collect, why we collect it, and how it may be used.

By using ShowUp you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app. ShowUp is intended for users 18 years of age and older.

What We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • First name and last initial
  • City and state
  • Age range
  • Gender
  • Career category
  • Hobbies and interests
  • Household type
  • Viewing habits (when you watch, how often, how you discover shows)
  • Email address (used for login only)

Information generated by your use of the app:

  • Shows you add to your Watching, Watchlist and Already Binged lists
  • Show ratings (1-5 remotes)
  • Comments and reactions left on shows
  • Streaming services associated with your shows
  • Friend connections within the app

Technical information:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage patterns and session data
  • IP address (used for security and fraud prevention only)

What Is Visible to Other Users

The following information is never visible to other users: your email address, age range, gender, career, hobbies, household type, viewing habits, and any other data collected on the Tell Us More screen.

The following information is visible to friends you connect with in your Living Room:

  • Your first name and last initial
  • Your city
  • Your Watching, Watchlist and Already Binged show lists
  • Your show ratings
  • Comments you leave on shows

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Operate and improve the ShowUp app and its features
  • Personalize your experience and show recommendations
  • Connect you with friends in your Living Room
  • Understand viewing trends across our user base
  • Improve the relevance of content and features we offer

Data Sharing and Third Parties

ShowUp may share anonymized and aggregated data with third party partners including streaming services, advertisers, content producers and market research firms. This means:

  • Your name, email, or any personally identifying information is never sold or shared
  • Data is combined and anonymized before sharing — no individual user can be identified
  • Examples of what may be shared: "ShowUp users aged 40-49 who list cooking as a hobby rate prestige dramas an average of 4.2 remotes"

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your personal data with advertisers for individual targeting.

We use Supabase to store your data securely. Supabase's privacy policy can be found at supabase.com/privacy.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion of your account and data we will remove your personal information within 30 days. Anonymized and aggregated data may be retained indefinitely as it contains no personally identifying information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Opt out of any future data sharing programs
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). To exercise any of these rights please contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

ShowUp is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18 we will promptly delete their account and data.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data including encrypted data storage, secure authentication and regular security reviews. However no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by posting a notice in the app. Continued use of ShowUp after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect when changes were last made.
